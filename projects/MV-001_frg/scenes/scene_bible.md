# MV场景设定集

## 场景1：出租屋/工位
- **场景ID**：scene_01
- **场景类型**：现实
- **场景描述**：狭窄的深夜出租屋兼工位。一张堆满草稿纸和技术书籍的桌子，一台亮着代码的显示器，一杯冒着微弱热气的咖啡。墙壁斑驳，空间逼仄，唯一光源是屏幕的蓝白色冷光。清晨版本：阳光透过窗帘缝隙洒入，桌面沐浴在温暖的晨光中。
- **出现段落**：SEG01, SEG02, SEG10
- **色调/氛围**：深夜版——冷蓝/灰黑，压抑孤独；清晨版——暖金/奶白，希望平静
- **关键视觉元素**：显示器蓝光、堆积的纸团、咖啡杯、逼仄空间、窗户（连接内外）
- **参考图来源**：generated
- **AI绘图关键词（英文）**：A cramped small apartment room serving as workspace, messy desk covered with crumpled papers and technical books, single computer monitor glowing with code, coffee cup with faint steam, peeling walls, claustrophobic space, dim blue screen light as only illumination at night version, warm golden morning sunlight version through curtain gaps, realistic urban apartment, four-angle reference sheet: front view, left 45 degrees, right 45 degrees, back view

## 场景2：城市街景
- **场景ID**：scene_02
- **场景类型**：现实
- **场景描述**：雨天的现代都市全景。密集的高楼大厦、交错的立交桥、车水马龙的街道。霓虹灯在雨幕中模糊散射。人群像蚂蚁般快速移动。后期版本（SEG09）：同一城市被金色光雨照亮后变得生机勃勃、色彩斑斓。
- **出现段落**：SEG02, SEG09
- **色调/氛围**：初始——冷灰/暗蓝，阴雨孤独；SEG09——金色光雨照亮后变为暖色生机
- **关键视觉元素**：立交桥、密集高楼、霓虹灯光晕、雨滴、快速移动的人群
- **参考图来源**：generated
- **AI绘图关键词（英文）**：A dense modern city skyline with towering skyscrapers and interchanging overpasses, rainy night with neon lights diffusing through rain, ant-like crowds moving rapidly on streets below, cold grey-blue desaturated tones with neon color bleeding, urban isolation atmosphere, also a golden-lit vibrant version where the same city is illuminated by falling golden light rain, four-angle reference: aerial view, street level, window POV looking out, wide establishing shot

## 场景3：地铁站
- **场景ID**：scene_03
- **场景类型**：现实（→过渡场景，在SEG03中碎裂）
- **场景描述**：拥挤的现代地铁站台。白色瓷砖墙、金属扶手、密集的人流。荧光灯发出刺眼的白光。这是现实世界与奇迹世界的穿越节点——在SEG03中，空间如玻璃般碎裂，露出底层的数字世界。
- **出现段落**：SEG03, SEG07（闪切）
- **色调/氛围**：冷白荧光+灰色调，压抑拥挤，碎裂时产生A→B的风格转换
- **关键视觉元素**：白色瓷砖墙、荧光灯管、金属扶手、拥挤人潮、碎裂裂纹
- **参考图来源**：generated
- **AI绘图关键词（英文）**：A crowded modern subway station platform with white tiled walls and metal handrails, harsh fluorescent overhead lighting, dense crowds of commuters, cold sterile atmosphere, grey desaturated tones, the space also shown shattering like glass with golden light visible through cracks, transition point between reality and digital world, four-angle reference: platform view, overhead view, passenger POV, shattering moment view

## 场景4：数字空间/赛博仙境
- **场景ID**：scene_04
- **场景类型**：幻想
- **场景描述**：由金色代码、霓虹蓝数据流和星辰构成的宏大数字神话空间。悬浮的代码高塔、流动的数据河流、漂浮的几何建筑碎片。空间充满失重感，粒子漂浮。这是MV中出现最频繁的场景，承载了所有"奇迹世界"的画面。
- **出现段落**：SEG04, SEG05, SEG06, SEG07, SEG08
- **色调/氛围**：鎏金色+深空蓝+霓虹，华丽史诗，失重感，赛博朋克×东方神话
- **关键视觉元素**：金色代码流、悬浮高塔、数据巨龙/星河、漂浮几何碎片、能量粒子
- **参考图来源**：generated
- **AI绘图关键词（英文）**：A vast digital mythology space constructed from golden code streams, neon blue data rivers, and stellar formations, featuring floating code towers, geometric architecture fragments, data dragon and galaxy shapes, energy particles drifting in zero gravity, epic cyber-mythology aesthetic blending Eastern mythology with cyberpunk, golden and deep space blue palette with neon accents, majestic scale and weightless atmosphere, four-angle reference: wide establishing shot, tower close-up, looking upward from below, panoramic vista

## 场景5：宇宙中心
- **场景ID**：scene_05
- **场景类型**：幻想
- **场景描述**：浩瀚宇宙的正中心，万千星辰环绕的空灵空间。巨大的发光"XWOW"文字标识悬浮在中央，四个字母由纯金色能量构成，散发温柔而耀眼的光芒。空间感极度开阔，具有神圣庄严的氛围。是数字空间的最深层，也是梦想具象化的终点。
- **出现段落**：SEG08, SEG09
- **色调/氛围**：温暖金光+深邃宇宙蓝黑，梦幻、神圣、温柔
- **关键视觉元素**：巨大发光XWOW文字标识、万千星辰、无限开阔宇宙空间、神圣光芒
- **参考图来源**：generated
- **AI绘图关键词（英文）**：The center of a vast cosmos surrounded by countless stars, a massive glowing XWOW text logo floating at the very center radiating warm sacred golden light, four bold letters made of pure golden energy, extremely open and infinite space, combination of warm golden radiance and deep cosmic blue-black, dreamlike and sacred atmosphere, majestic and tender, stars swirling gently, four-angle reference: frontal view of XWOW logo, side perspective showing cosmic scale, looking through from behind, close-up of glowing letter surface
